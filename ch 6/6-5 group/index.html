About Us

My papa Louie Bianchi grew up an orphan on the streets of Sicily, Italy. In a time when there was famine and corruption my papa and the brothers he had made at the orphanage had dreams to see, explore, and make something of themselves. So after tireless traviling and near giving up moments they finally built their small restaurnat in Palermo.

Soon after he met my mother and they had me and my 4 other siblings.

Together we have been running this restaurant for over 70 years!

We serve the best pizza in town! We pride ourselves on using the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes to create mouthwatering pizzas that will satisfy your cravings. We take pride in getting good reviews from our customers and being able to to take care of them in ways only your favorite pizzeria can!
